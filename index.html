Narrative Architecture Website Series
Narrative Architecture Website Series is an ongoing web project initiated in 2018 by Yehwan Song. It rejects standardized web templates in favor of structures generated from content itself. Rather than imposing a predefined layout, the site’s architecture emerges from the logic, relationships, and sequencing within the material.

Navigation is not prescribed. The user is required to explore—moving through the site as one would move through a spatial system, uncovering connections rather than following a fixed path. The project operates as an ongoing attempt to rethink the internet as a constructed environment rather than a neutral interface.

The approach moves away from:
heavy, monumental structures.
rigid planning systems.
permanence as a design goal.


Instead, it favors:
flexibility over fixed form.
temporality over permanence.
dynamic, technological systems over static layouts.
user-driven exploration over imposed navigation.
